Who am I?

Hi, my name is Valentin Thevoz but everyone just calls me Val. I was born in Switzerland and spent 9 precious years of my life there. I quickly moved to London on a mission to learn English and as of 2016 I have been living in Austin Texas, where I really found myself. Living in so many different cultures has taught me how to learn and adapt to different cultures, races, and religions.
